The game my partner and I picked for task 1 was battleship. We picked battleship because we were both familiar with the game and were quickly able to identify the necessary answers for the task.

Answers

What actions can I take?

  • Arrange your own 5 ships on a 10x10 grid, and leave them for the remainder of the game.
  • Attempt to sink an opponent's ship by calling out squares where you think the enemy's ships are hiding.
  • Fire a bomb at a ship.

    • What do I need to remember to keep track of?

  • Where you have already hit a boat.
  • Player with the red kit goes first.

    • What do I do over and over again?

  • Take turns calling out squares.
  • Continue the loop until all sections and ships have been hit.

    When do I need to make decisions?

  • When it is your turn, after you hit a ship, where to hit next to take down the ship.

    • Pseudocode Steps

  • Place your boats on your side.
  • Once both opponents have placed boats, the player with the red kit goes first and guesses the location/square of where they think the ship is.
  • If a ship is in that square, it is hit, if it is not, then it is not hit.
  • Repeat until all of a player's ships are hit meaning they lost.
